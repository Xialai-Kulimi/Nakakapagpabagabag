### 2.side=LEFT` It's mean objects will start placing from **left** to **right**.
```python
# 以下為 top 群組
left_button = tk.Button(top_frame, text='Red', fg='red')
# 讓系統自動擺放元件，預設為由上而下（靠左）
left_button.pack(side=tk.LEFT)
```
